2) Conclusions¶

The best parameter for the model is gamma equal to 7¶

It seems that in this case we deal with overfitting problem, the difference in the R square score between the testand trainsamples, which is significant bigger than in the rest of the models¶

This could be due to the high selected gamma parameters, as it can be seen in the graph. However, reducing the gamma from 7 to 5 will not deal in a better model than the obtained in boosting¶

In order to compare the models R squared score of the test sample will be taken, which is equal to 85,48%¶
